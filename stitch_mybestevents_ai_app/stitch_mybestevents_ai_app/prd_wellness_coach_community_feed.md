# MyBestEvents - Wellness Coach & Community PRD

## 1. Feature Overview: AI Wellness Coach
A chat-based AI assistant that acts as a personal concierge. It uses the user's mood, history, and the 7 Wellness Spheres to suggest and book activities.

### Key Capabilities:
- **Proactive Suggestions**: "I see you've had a stressful morning. Would you like to book a 15-minute guided meditation at the Zen Center nearby?"
- **Natural Language Booking**: "Find me a healthy lunch spot in Montpellier for today at 1 PM."
- **Mood Tracking Integration**: Analyzes user check-ins to offer personalized recovery paths.

## 2. Feature Overview: Community Feed
A social space to drive engagement, social proof, and virality within the wellness ecosystem.

### Key Capabilities:
- **Wellness Moments**: Users can share photos/videos of their experiences.
- **Follow Wellness Leaders**: Following local influencers and top-rated partners.
- **Local Groups & Challenges**: "30-day Yoga Challenge in Béziers."
- **Social Proof**: Seeing where friends have booked and their reviews.

## 3. Design Direction
- **Conversational UX**: The Coach interface should feel like a messaging app (clean, responsive, empathetic).
- **Visual Storytelling**: The Community feed should be image/video-heavy (TikTok/Instagram style) to build desire.
- **Integration**: Seamless transition from a community post to a booking via the Coach.

## 4. Technical Logic
- **Cerveau Central Integration**: The Coach is the front-end for the AI decision engine we've previously structured.
- **Engagement Loop**: Post -> Clicks -> IA Analysis -> Personalized Coach Suggestion.
