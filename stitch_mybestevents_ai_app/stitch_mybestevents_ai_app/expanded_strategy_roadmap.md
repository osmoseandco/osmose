# MyBestEvents - Strategic Expansion & Roadmap (New Insights)

## 1. Core Vision Update
MyBestEvents is evolving from a wellness marketplace into a **Personal Wellness Concierge & Community Hub**. The goal is to provide a seamless, AI-driven lifestyle companion that not only recommends but actively manages a user's wellness journey.

## 2. New Strategic Pillars
- **Personalized Concierge**: AI that proactively suggests activities based on mood, schedule, and historical behavior (e.g., "You've had a busy week; here's a meditation session nearby").
- **Community & Social Proof**: Strengthening the social aspect by allowing users to share experiences, follow "wellness leaders," and join local groups.
- **Deep Integration**: Moving beyond simple bookings to integrated wellness tracking (mood, energy, social engagement).

## 3. Advanced Feature Roadmap
- **AI Wellness Coach**: A chat-based assistant that can answer questions and book activities on the fly.
- **Dynamic Pricing & Flash Offers**: Allowing partners to fill empty slots with real-time discounts.
- **Influencer-Led Experiences**: Exclusive events hosted by top wellness influencers in the Occitanie region.
- **Subscription Tiers**: Enhancing the "User Subscription" with perks like priority booking and exclusive content.

## 4. Growth & Scale (The 10K/mo Path)
- **Local Dominance**: Perfecting the Béziers/Montpellier hub before rapid expansion to Toulouse and Lyon.
- **Automation at Scale**: Leveraging n8n to handle partner onboarding and lead generation across multiple regions simultaneously.
- **Community Growth**: Using "Viral TikTok Loops" specifically focused on hidden local gems and wellness transformations.

## 5. Design & UX Direction
- **Hyper-Personalization**: The interface should feel unique to every user.
- **Low Friction**: One-click bookings and seamless calendar sync.
- **Visual Storytelling**: Using high-quality video and imagery from influencers to build desire.
